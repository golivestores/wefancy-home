Our Beginning

Designed with purpose.
Made for every body.

It started with a problem we knew too well: during pregnancy, there was no pillow that actually worked. The ones on the market were either too bulky, too hot, or fell apart in weeks. We set out to build something better.

Three years, 47 prototypes, and hundreds of conversations with real moms later — we had our answer. A pillow that supports you in every position, stays cool all night, and lasts well beyond those nine months.
